Our WEXi programme...
WEXi (Work EXperience initiative) – enabling young people to gain life skills & move on into sustainable employment…
We have recently developed WEXi (Work EXperience initiative) by partnering with local businesses, organisations and charities to provide an outcome-based programme of employability skills to enable the young people we support to move on into employment.

Our current WEXi programme providers are:

HiQ: Tyres and Autocare Gosport – garage – mechanical, valeting and repair skills;



Gosport Museum & Art Gallery (GMAG) – heritage & culture – café, front of house and general duty skills;

The CO-OPerative – offering a variety of retail, customer and grocery skills;

RSPCA: Stubbington Ark – a large animal centre, offering care for a wide range of domestic pets, farm animals and wildlife;

The Carisbrooke Arms – a local Gosport pub – bar/kitchen, hospitality skills and general duties;

Nelly’s Woofers & Grooming: Fareham – offering dog grooming, alongside selling natural & raw foods, treats & accessories;

Barnados Charity Shop – various public service skills within a retail environment;

Plan B Alternative Provision –  ship building, music workshops, training and water sports (this is currently on hold);

Marvels & Meltdowns – supporting the staff, children & the families at events within the centre;

Community Allotment – horticulture – weeding, watering, planting, pruning, harvesting, plus much more (this is in development);

Gosport Leisure Centre – offering a variety of sport & leisure facilities, including swimming lessons, a gym, plus more! (this is in development);

The Hub4stuff – furniture collection for redistribution and re-purposing – customer service and community engagement;

Gosport Community Association – gardening, decorating, administration, bar work and event management;

Gosport Community Cinema – social media, event management and sound desk skills;

Open Sight, Gosport – who provide guidance, support and activities for visually impaired individuals – supporting meetings, administrative skills, befriending;

We have more partnerships interested in becoming involved with WEXi – watch this space for more updates…

Our charity’s ethos is to provide our youth volunteers with a social pathway into employment, that is cost effective and eventually not reliant on grant funding.
