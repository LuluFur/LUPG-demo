We are Level Up Gosport!
Who we are: Level Up Gosport (LUPG) is a focused, local charity dedicated to empowering young people (aged 18-25) facing additional needs, mental health challenges, anxiety, or SEND. We bridge societal gaps by providing tailored support, fostering confidence, reducing isolation and promoting personal growth through mentorship, team engagement, community volunteering, alongside our outcome-based Work EXperience initiative (WEXi) programmes.

Our Vision: Our strategic aim is to enable these young individuals to transition to independence, form meaningful friendships, and secure sustainable employment opportunities.  

Our Mission: We believe in the limitless potential of young people, seeing them as key to a brighter future for our community. We are committed to equity and inclusion, ensuring that everyone, regardless of identity, background, or disability, has the opportunity to reach their full potential, strengthening our community.

Our charity offers young people a range of impactful opportunities to develop their skills, build confidence, and thrive:

WEXi (Work EXperience initiative): A free, innovative and diverse programme of supported, outcome-focused work experience;
Volunteering: Engaging in local community activities and events to promote social inclusion and personal growth;
Social Meetings: Fortnightly gatherings to foster friendships, reduce isolation and build a supportive community;
Training & Workshops: Skills-based sessions on topics such as Money Matters, Health & Safety, Safeguarding and Food Health & Hygiene to enhance employability and life skills;
Community Allotment: Hands-on experience in cultivating an organic, sustainable allotment, learning horticultural skills while promoting environmental responsibility.
